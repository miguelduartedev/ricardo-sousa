Ricardo Sousa (b. Cascais 1993) is a designer and photographer currently based in Lisbon, Portugal.

He holds a bachelor degree in Equipment Design (2013-16) from the Faculty of Fine Arts in Lisbon.

His approach is characterized by an alchemic, experimental and multidisciplinary nature within a meticulous and high-attention-to-detail aesthetic.

He recently participated in a In-Residence Workshop #14 with the theme of Truth-Tellers hosted by Maat Museum with the design-tutors Soft Baroque and Thomas Ballouhey.

Full portfolio and CV upon request.

All pieces are available to order.
