Ricardo Sousa is a designer and photographer based in Cascais, Portugal. In 2016 he completed his degree at Faculdade de Belas-Artes of the Universidade de Lisboa.

He currently works between the fields of design & photography.  His work is characterised by an experimental & multidisciplinary nature within a meticulous and high-detail focused aesthetic. He is now researching & developing pieces around portuguese & international marble.

Full portfolio and cv available upon request.
